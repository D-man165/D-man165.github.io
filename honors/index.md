## George Gregson Scholarship in Physics, 2026

A scholarship endowment established by George Gregson of Beverly Hills, California. Recognized in the Department of Physics with an exceptional academic record a cumulative GPA of atleast 3.5.

## Galileo Circle Scholar, 2026

Recognition for academic excellence. The Galileo Circle awards scholarships to outstanding undergraduate and graduate students who show exceptional potential in the physical, mathematical, environmental, cognitive, or life sciences.

## US-ATLAS ATC, 2026

Awarded funding to support research with the ATLAS experiment at Brookhaven National Laboratory.

## Global Wildcat Award, Fall 2024 - present
